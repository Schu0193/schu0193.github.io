Where does it come from?

Hogwarts was first created and introduced from the ever popular books series Harry Potter. It is the main setting for many of the novels and is included as a reference throughout many other stories. The author J.K. Rowling, has written and created many novels and plays tied to this world. Pictures 4 and 5 show the original book series and the series title.


Books within this universe include:

1. Harry Potter and The Philosopher’s Stone
2. Harry Potter and The Chamber of Secrets
3. Harry Potter and The Prisoner Of Azkaban
4. Harry Potter and The Goblet of Fire
5. Harry Potter and The Order of The Phoenix
6. Harry Potter and The Half-Blood Prince
7. Harry Potter and The Deathly Hallows
8. The Cursed Child
9. The Tales of Beetle The Bard
10. Fantastic Beast and Where to Find Them


What We Offer

We pride ourselves in offering thee complete experience. You will be able to enjoy becoming a student within the school and practicing magic. Be sorted into the correct house, meet many of the amazing creatures, as well as all the professors and characters. This is an experience frozen in time that many seek after once they finish all of the previously available material.
